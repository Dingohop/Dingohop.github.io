Accessibility statement

Taylor’s Design Media is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, including people who use assistive technology.

Our accessibility approach

This website has been designed to support the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA. Accessibility features include:

  • Keyboard-accessible navigation and video controls
  • Visible focus indicators and a skip link
  • Color contrast designed for readable text and controls
  • Responsive layouts that support text enlargement and reflow
  • Reduced motion support based on device preferences
  • Descriptive labels for links, controls, and embedded video players

Video accessibility

Portfolio videos are hosted by YouTube. Captions are requested by default when they are available. If you need a transcript or another accessible format for a video, please contact us.

Feedback and assistance

If you experience an accessibility barrier or need content in another format, email michaeltaylor82@gmail.com. Please include the page or video involved and the type of assistance you need.
